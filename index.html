SPOMINI

Prevoje, Brdo, Št Vid

Memories

Vera Terstenjak

Multidimenzionalno okno spominov. Akril-olje na platnu, 100 cm x 100 cm, 2014.
Multidimenzionalno okno spominov, kjer se srečajo preteklost, sedanjost in prihodnost. Lik moje matere Josie, njenega očeta Franka in mojega brata Nika.

A multidimensional window of memories where the past, the present & the future meet... The image of my mother Josie, her father Frank and my brother Nick.

Hiša mojega rojstva - Prevoje 3. Akril-olje na platnu, 100 cm x 100 cm, 2015.
Nekje v času in prostoru stoji hiša mojega rojstva - Prevoje 3. To okolje je dalo meni, moji sestri in mojemu bratu veliko domišljiske svobode ob odkrivanju življenskih izkušenj.

Somewhere in time and space the house of my birth at Prevoje 3. These surroundings gave me, my sister and my brother much imaginative freedom in discovering life's challenges...

Roza spomini. Akril-olje na platnu, 100 cm x 100 cm, 2014.
Roza spomini zgodnjega otroštva. Hiša rojstva, kuku ura, cerkev Sv. Vida- mesto krščenja, kozolec kjer se ure domišljije za nas otroke niso nikoli končale. Muca Lady Loydica, glavna spremljevalka ob trenutkih usamljenosti slikarke.

Pink memories of the early artist's childhood. The house of birth, the cuckoo clock, St Vid's church the place of Baptizing, the hayloft where many imaginative hours for us children never ended. The cat Lady Loydica which was a great companion when the artist was lonesome...

Posvečeno omami Elzi. Akril-olje na platnu, 100 cm x 100 cm, 2015.
Rjavi Steinway klavir je omogočil sanjsko vzdušje, ki ga je ustvarila omama Elza in njeni prstni udarci po klaviaturi. Te trenutke nosim v sebi, ko želim biti v nadrealni dimenziji sedanjega...kuku ura, stene Kersnikovega gradu... linearni čas...

The brown Steinway piano that made life seem like a dream with Grandma Elza's hands on the keyboard. These moments I carry within me whenever I want to be in the surreal dimension of the now...of course the cuckoo clock & the walls of Kersnik castle are there to register the linear time.

Vrata do novih spoznanj so v nas samih. Olje na platnu, 100 cm X 100 cm, 2016.
Vrata do novih spoznanj so v nas samih, še posebno, ko smo uglašeni z univerzalnim zavedanjem o enosti.

One can always find the portal to new discoveries within oneself when in tune with the universal awareness of oneness...

V tišini časa in prostora...

In the stillness of time and space...

Kersnikov grad skozi roza tančico. Olje na platnu, 100 cm x 100 cm, 2015.
Kersnikov grad skozi roza tančico časa... v njem je služboval opapa Leo kot visoki uradnik po 1.sv. vojni. Kasneje je, do 2. sv. vojne, prav tu in prav tako kot visoki uradnik, deloval tudi moj oče Niko.

Kersnik castle through a pink veil. Grandfather Leo worked in this castle as a higher court official after WW I. My father Nick also worked there in same capacity until WW ll.

Podoživljanje otroških spominov. Akril-olje na platnu, 100 cm x 100 cm, 2016.
Podoživljanje otroških spominov... lik mojega očeta v otroški podobi, obe stavbi sta bili pod okriljem starih staršev, dramatičen požar, ki je prizadel sosede... vodnjak mladosti...

Recreation of childhood memories... The image of my father as a child, two houses that were owned by my grandparent, traumatic fire that broke out at our nearby neighbors...the fountain of youth...

Sanje o sanjah. Akril-olje na platnu, 100 cm x 100 cm, 2014.
Sanje o sanjah. Mamina mati Margareta Luteršek, New York 1910, ko je bila krščena moja mati Josie. Drevesa... kot odnos med nebom in zemljo ter ženski in moški princip.

Dreaming the dream. My mother's mother Margaret Lutersek . New York 1910, when my Mother Josie was Baptized. Two trees symbolize the relationship between the earth and the sky or woman and man...

Drevo življenja. Akril-olje na platnu, 100 cm x 100 cm, 2014.
Drevo življenja. Simbol življenja, smrti in preporod skozi trajanje časa in prostora...

The tree of life. Symbolizing life, death and rebirth throughout time and space...

From one world to another through the portal of cosmic time. Inspired by the ruins of the Brdo castle.

Belonging. The roots that cannot be concealed. Images of Peter and Phillip as representing the past and a premonition of the future.
